About

Valorant is a game that is made by riot games. The creator of also League of Legends.
Inside Valorant's home screen a user would see a navigation bar at the top of the screen, a vertical bar on the right
where they can add or join a friend's party. Lastly, a type of news source on the left that talks about the game's new updates.
At the navigation bar, users can expect a "Play button" in the middle, as well as, the buttons: career, collection and store on
the right side of it, and home, battlepass, agents on the left side. The home button would lead a person to the menu screen,
the battlepass would lead to rewards people can recieve if they would purchase it, the agents button would lead them to
a gallery of playble characters, the career option would illustrate a user's statistics of their performance, as well as,
their rank, the collection button would lead the user to a page that shows the weapons that users can use in-game. Lastly, the
store button allows users to purchase cosmetics for their weapons. In addition, the purchasable cosmetics reset after a certain
amount of time. This action renews the store, which presents people with different type of cosmetics. To add on, the last main
button is Play, which leads to a screen where they can join a game.














Gameplay

After pressing the Play button, people are able to choose different type of gamemodes, as well as, invite as many
as 4 teammates which fill the whole roster of five people. However, if a user decides to make a custom game then
they can invite as many as 9 people, since a full game consists of ten people, five on opposing sides. The most active
gamemodes in Valorant are unrated and rank. In unrated people can play anyway they want without having their rank affected,
however, in rank people play more competitively as the winner of game increases their rank. The ranks are iron, bronze, silver,
gold, platinum, diamond, immortal, and radiant. Iron being the lowest and radiant the highest. Your rank is decided by how much
MMR (Matchmaking Rank) you have, and to increase MMR you have to win and perform well in rank games. Once you are in a game, you
go through agent picking phase, where you and your teammates choose what agents you want to play, however, two teammates cannot play
the same agent. As a result it is good to decide on who you want to play before it gets to that phase. After that, you are dropped
in a random map that has labeled sites. If you are on the defending side, then you are responsible for defending a site from attackers.
As a defender you have three types of way to win. First way is to eliminate all attackers, second way is to prevent the attacking side
from planting a bomb and wait until the timer runs out, and the third way is to eliminate all attackers after the bomb has been planted
and to then defuse it before it explodes. However, if you are an attacker, you are to decide on a site as a team and make a strategy to
either eliminate all defenders or plant a bomb and defend it, or both to win the round. If you win a round then you get one point, to
win the whole game you need to gain 13 points (13 rounds). Additionally, at every start of the game you start with a certain amount
of currency. With this currency, players can purchase weapons, however each weapon have different value which is based on their
effectiveness and firepower. To earn that type of currency, users would have to win rounds and get kills, however, you can also
gain them, but in small amounts, through just completing rounds.
