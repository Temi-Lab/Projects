Company - Riot Games



















Valorant was made by a company named Riot Games. Which is very famous for the creation of League of Legends.
After, only focusing on League of Legends for 11 years, Riot Games has decided to broaden their range and have
introduced the game Valorant. At the beginning, it had recieved a lot of critisism for just being a copy of
CSGO, which was another FPS shooter that was very loved in the FPS community. However, as time went by people
began to see the differences, and, on the contrary, believe it was going to replace CSGO. As it recieved more
support and attention, they decided to create tournaments, which had a lot of viewers. Recieving money from
League of Legends and Valorant, they have decided to also create a Netflix series based on League of Legends'
lore. Which suprisingly got great ratings and scores. With hacing a successful year, Riot games have already
introduced more games in which they are currently working on. Additionally, Riot games have also collabed with
various musicians and groups, like Imagine Dragons. As it stands, Riot games currently has a large fanbase and
support, unlike their competitors like Blizzard.
